Our ingredients for sustainability

We’ve baked in sustainability from the start. It requires a trifecta partnership between philanthropy, government and parents.

Our trifecta partnership

Government supports F4E directly, by paying for 230,000 meals per day — 68% of total meals currently served.

Philanthropic funding has stoked our vision, investing in our operational model as we R&D, amp up our scale, and drive down our cost per meal.

Parents tap in with their own contribution. We want every parent to buy a healthy, safe, delicious school lunch their kids love. Payment amounts vary by location and ability.

Spotlighting Dishi na County

“Dishi na County” is a partnership between Nairobi City County and the Ministry of Education executed by Food 4 Education.

The program ensures the provision of a nutritionally balanced, hot meal in every school day for children in public primary schools and public Early Childhood Development (ECD) centers in Nairobi City County. Want to learn more? Check out the FAQ on the Nairobi County website.
