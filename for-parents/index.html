Get Started

Register

Fill out the form from your child’s school.

02

Top Up Your Account

  1. Go to M-PESA
  2. Select Lipa na M-PESA
  3. Go to paybill
  4. Input paybill number — 956781
  5. Select to input the account number
  6. Input your registered Tap2Eat phone number
  7. Input the amount to top up for all your children
  8. Submit the information
  9. Input PIN and wait for confirmation message.

Tap & Eat

Your child will tap their wristband at lunch time, and enjoy a nutritious meal!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tap2Eat?

Tap2Eat is a cashless payment system that makes it easy for parents to financially contribute towards meals for children.

What is a Tap2Eat wristband?

A Tap2Eat wristband is a device that looks like a watch that is given to the children to wear on their wrist. It is used to deduct meal payments during each feeding time. Children tap their watch on a device, the amount is deducted from their account, and they can access their meals.

How does my child get a Tap2Eat wristband?

Once your account is registered, your child will receive a wristband for free.

What happens when my child loses her/his Tap2Eat wristband?

The first wristband is free. Report any lost Tap2Eat wristband to Tap2Eat representatives in your child’s school. You can reach out to the Tap2Eat customer experience team through 0713379230 for a replacement.

How can I make payments or top-up my Tap2Eat account?

To top-up your account
Go to Mpesa — Lipa na Mpesa — Select paybill — Business number 956781 — Account number — Tap2Eat account number — Input the Amount — Input Pin — Confirm transaction.
You will receive a confirmation message that shows that your money has been deposited into the Food for Education Foundation paybill.

How do I view my Tap2Eat account details?

Dial *648# on your Safaricom registered number to view the children registered under your account and your account balance.

Can I send my child with the lunch money or Tap2Eat wristband replacement payment to school?

No, the Tap2Eat program is cashless and does not accept any cash payments for Tap2Eat wristband replacements and/or food.

How much are meals?

The prices vary depending on the area and program your child is in. Pricing per meal is communicated per school during the school onboarding process.

How can I make payments if my sim card or phone gets lost?

Payment into your account can be made using any other Safaricom phone number provided that you input your already registered number as the account number.

What happens to the money in my account if my child misses school or lunch for a day or two?

Your money remains in your account until meal consumption happens. Deduction from your account only happens when your child is tapped and actually served meals, otherwise, your account remains untampered.

Where can I present my feedback or queries on meals, Tap2Eat wristband, payment issue etc?

You can reach out to the Tap2Eat customer experience team through 0713379230.
