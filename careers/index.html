Our internship and externship programs connect students from across the globe with hands-on, impact-driven projects. Medical Insurance

Taking care of our team is about more than what happens in the office. The company offers medical coverage for all the staff together with their dependants

Annual Mental Health Break

A mental health break could be as simple as taking an hour to meditate or a day off from the stress of work. Taking a mental health break is important for recuperating and avoiding burnout. At F4E we have employee's well being at the center of our hearts hence the provision of the Annual Mental Health break. A week of rejuvenating and re-energizing away from work for all employees. Total shut down of operations!

Office Food Program

Employees have one less thing for to Stress About in the office. That haunting question of what am I gonna have for lunch tomorrow? can be a source of unneeded stress at work and our office food program relieves all our employees of that stress.

Recognition

Recognition motivates people, which boost teamwork and productivity. It improves your workplace culture, creating a supportive work environment. It helps retain top talent and reduce staff turnover It instills a sense of purpose and makes your business more value-oriented. We celebrate birthdays and anniversaries together.
