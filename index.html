FOOD AND EDUCATION FOR KIDS - Feeding the future .

Hungry kids can’t learn.

They don’t have the strength, attention span, or interest. Parents want their children to have hot, affordable, and nutritious lunches. Government leaders want to create a new norm. But a blueprint for school feeding programs is nonexistent or unscalable, stunting the prosperity that our children deserve.

Explore our why
The big idea

The problem is big.
We can’t afford to think small.

School feeding programs unlock the potential of a generation. So we’ve created the blueprint for an efficient and affordable program that delivers nutritious meals at scale. We’re proving it's possible in Kenya. Creating an example that can expand across all of Africa.

01

Our work isEfficient.

We build centralized kitchens. We pair tech-enabled operations with a staunch dedication to optimize costs, improve operational efficiency, create transparency, and deliver delicious, high-quality meals.

02

Our food isNutritious.

Our meals are nutrient-dense, featuring a high protein to carb ratio. We use locally-sourced seasonal vegetables, harvested at their peak. We cook them in verified safe kitchens, and serve them to children while still hot.

03

Daily lunch becomesAffordable.

We’ve kept quality high and costs down, so we can offer a daily meal for $0.30 or less. Made possible by an obsessive focus on gaining efficiencies in every step. From field to fork.
