Help us feed 1 million children every day

Join our Founders' Circle Donate through your DAF

Thanks to the amazing support of our partner foundations, 100% of all individual donations are tax deductible and go directly to sourcing ingredients from farmers, preparing meals, and feeding children. Food for Education Foundation, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization, EIN 88-0664918.

Hungry kids can’t learn. Going without lunch hinders educational outcomes and obstructs children’s physical, cognitive, and social-emotional development. Which limits the potential of a generation. 

By 2050, 1 in 4 people will be African. Without a sustainable solution, over 800 million children will struggle due to classroom hunger.

For the past decade we’ve been supported by philanthropic foundations and corporations which have fueled our growth from feeding 25 to over 500,000 kids on a daily basis. Now we need to widen our village with more cooks in the kitchen if we’re to realize our audacious goal of feeding 1 million children per day.
